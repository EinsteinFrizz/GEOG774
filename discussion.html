Discussion

It can be seen in both Method and Results that the analysis performed on the data collected at Tatapouri was manual and largely superficial.
It was originally planned to use a method much more like Shazam, involving Fourier analysis of the wave spectrum and using Python code to automatically compare waves across the whole dataset. An initial attempt of this method produced the spectrogram below.

The conclusion of this study was that the spectral analysis of water waves, specificall infragravity waves, on a shore platform is a complicated task. The programming required to make this a viable study is out of the scope of the semester-long project that GEOG 330 allows, and requires in-depth knowledge of various elements of physics and coding.
